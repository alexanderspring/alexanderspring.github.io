Biography

Alex was born and raised in New York City by his single mother.
There, he was able to become independent and do what he had to do to be successful
on the road to obtaining his lifelong dream, becoming a physician. Alex has six years of
research experience in both the biological and psychological sciences. Alex is a nationally
certified and Massachusetts state licenced EMT and works for an ambulance company part time.
He is also currently writing a manuscript in the Nutrition and Behavior Lab at Tufts University.
In his free time Alex DJs live events and records live mixes. Alex also is an avid swimmer and enjoys
photography and cooking.
